About Me

My name is Emin Aksoy, at the time of writing I am 19 years old and am currently a first year undergraduate student studying Computer Science at University College Cork. My interests broadly include computing, photography and history.

I was born in Cork in 2004, however I have lived most of my life in Enniscorthy, Co. Wexford. I first attended St. Aidan's Parish School, I then attended what was then known as Enniscorthy Vocational College, though the school's name has since changed to Enniscorthy Community College. Once I finished there, I did a QQI Level 5 course in Computer Science so that I could attend University College Cork.

Work Experience

Blue Escape Dive Center - 2019

In the summer of 2019, I did volunteer work at Blue Escape Dive Center in Bodrum, Turkey. I did this volunteer work because I was working on getting a SCUBA diving certification at the time and I took advantage of the opportunity to do lots of diving experience by working with the staff there, since the dive center also brought tourists on daily dive trips. I acted as an assistant to the dive leaders, and as a Turkish - English interpreter.

Gadget Technology Enniscorthy - 2022-2023

For the duration of my QQI course, part of the curriculum was that I had to do work experience in an ICT job. I applied to various offices, shops, and even my former secondary school; I could not find work anywhere, except at Gadget Technology in Enniscorthy. I worked there for 1 day per week at first, and then 3 days per week later on in my course. During my tenure there I primarily repaired computers and phones while also working with the general public. At the end of my tenure there, even though it was volunteer work, I still received pay from the proprietor of the business.

Aksoy Jewellers - Current

I am currently working part-time in the family business, Aksoy Jewellers in Ballincollig, Co. Cork. I will likely remain here for the duration of my degree, and also for my Master's Degree, when I eventually do that. Here I help to sell jewellery to the public. We primarily specialise in wedding jewellery, and in 2015 and 2019 we received awards for said wedding jewellery.
